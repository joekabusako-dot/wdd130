Funny Splash Rafting Company

At Funny Splash Rafting Company, our goal is to provide unforgettable and safe river adventures for everyone. We are committed to creating exciting experiences that bring people closer to nature while building teamwork, confidence, and lasting memories.

History

Funny Splash Company was founded in 2010 along the rushing rivers of the Congo. What started as a small group of rafting enthusiasts quickly grew into a company known for its friendly spirit and love of adventure. For more than a decade, we have welcomed thousands of visitors, sharing with them our passion for nature, excitement, and unforgettable experiences.

Adventure Awaits You!

At Funny Splash Company, every trip is a unique adventure. Our experienced guides lead you through thrilling rapids, breathtaking scenery, and moments you will never forget.

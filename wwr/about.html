Funny splash Rafting Company

At Funny Splash Rafting Company, our goal is to provide unforgettable and safe river adventures for everyone. We are committed to creating exciting experiences that bring people closer to nature while building teamwork, confidence, and lasting memories.

History

Funny Splash Rafting Company started as a small group of adventure lovers who enjoyed fun “splish-splash” river rides. What began as a simple “whooshy” idea quickly grew into a company dedicated to exciting and safe rafting experiences. Today, we continue to share our love for “giggle-water” adventures with everyone.

Congo-Kinshasa

The Democratic Republic of the Congo, also called Congo-Kinshasa, is a country in Central Africa known for its vast rainforests, rich natural resources, and diverse cultures. With the Congo River flowing through it, the country is home to incredible wildlife, vibrant traditions, and a history that shapes its unique identity. From bustling cities like Kinshasa to the natural beauty of national parks, Congo-Kinshasa offers a rich mix of culture, nature, and heritage.
